__Programmable VFO__

Set:

	PV p1,p2,p3

Get:

	PV p1

returns: p1,p2,p3

|p1|function|
|---|---|
|0|A band 136
|1|A band 410
|2|A band 118
|3|B band 136
|4|B band 320
|5|B band 400

|p2|function|
|---|---|
|nnnn|Lower frequency in MHz. 4 digit

|p3|function|
|---|---|
|nnnn|Upper frequency in MHz. 4 digit
